    The Mont-Pierre Central London

    The Mont-Pierre has a reputation as one of the best restaurants in London, and with good reason. It’s more than just a great place to eat out – it’s an institution.

    Enjoy a delicious lunch, intimate dinner, or one of our indulgent Sunday brunches. Our menus cater for everyone, and include great options for vegetarians and vegans. No reservation? No problem. You’re welcome at our central dining bar where you can enjoy a glass of wine or a cocktail from our dedicated menu.

    Fine Dining in Covent Garden, London

    Seeing a show or headed for an evening out? Then look no further. The Mont-Pierre is perfectly placed for you to enjoy a delicious meal before your night out. We’re just around the corner from West End stalwarts the Piccadilly and Cambridge Theatres, as well as the famous Palace Theatre.

    We want you to have the very best experience possible, and so we are still implementing the highest standards of health and safety to ensure guests and employees are able to dine and work in confidence and in a safe environment. Further information can be found here.

    Dress Code

    Our dress code is smart casual. Tailored shorts and short sleeved shirts are permitted. However guests are asked to be respectful of the fact that many diners will be dressed for a celebration.

    Central Dining Bar

    The Mont-Pierre welcomes customers without reservations at the central dining bar, for lunch and dinner. The bar is not licensed for drinks without food.
